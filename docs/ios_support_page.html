How accurate is the plant identification?
Plant Forager uses advanced AI with dual-modal recognition (leaf and flower photos) for high accuracy. However, always verify with multiple sources and never consume any plant without 100% positive identification.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! Plant Forager includes offline mode so you can identify plants even in remote locations without internet access. The comprehensive database is stored locally on your device.
How do I use the safety warnings feature?
When identifying a plant, look for the red warning icons that indicate toxic look-alikes. Always read the full safety information before considering any plant for consumption.
Can I get location-specific plant recommendations?
Yes! Enable location services to get recommendations for edible plants native to your area and see what's in season through our seasonal calendar feature.
How do I take the best photos for identification?
For best results, take clear, well-lit photos of both leaves and flowers. Capture the whole plant if possible, including stems and growth pattern. Avoid shadows and blurry images.
Is it safe for beginners to use this app?
Plant Forager is designed with safety first, featuring toxic look-alike warnings and expert-verified information. However, beginners should also consult field guides and experienced foragers before consuming any wild plants.
